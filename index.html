ABOUT ME

brian toombs

Graphic Designer and Web Developer

Over my career, my rich experience has run the gamut from production artist to art director, and more often than not, all in the same day. I have been fortunate to work on a variety of projects ranging from print and logo development (like the WFYI logo, among many), to tradeshow, signage, web, email and multimedia design. Recently, I have been working to add web development to my skill set.

Are you looking for a seasoned graphic designer, or perhaps an art director, with web development capabilities? Does your team lack someone able to elevate your output to agency-level standards? Do you crave long, empassioned discussions of music, movies, and various other pop culture topics, coupled with a quick wit and penchant for dad jokes? Yeah? Really?!?! Great! Drop me an email in the contact section below.

I have devoted a quarter of a century to my craft, and plan to continue for another quarter or two. When I'm not working, I'm usually working more—freelance keeps me busy. I've also performed in bands over the years, and occasionally pick up my harmonica to see what I've forgotten or grab a mic and give the old vocal cords a workout. I have a wonderful wife who insists I dance with her when the right band is playing—sometimes on the dancefloor, sometimes in the kitchen, and always once or twice more than I think I have in me. I have a son and two stepsons who keep me busy, worried, broke, proud, and happy; and a dog who likes to nestle in the crook of my leg.
